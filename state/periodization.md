# Periodization Plan — Spartan Ultra Morzine 2027

## Phase Overview

### Phase 1: The Reconstruction (Jan - Mar 2026) ← CURRENT
- **Focus:** Structural integrity, metabolic reset, joint armor, fat loss. Upper body plyo introduction. Pull-up progression.
- **Training:** Gym-based strength 4-5x/week. No running (treadmill incline walking begins late Phase 1). Rower for conditioning. Stairmaster for aerobic threshold. Core stability 3x/week.
- **Weight Target:** ≤96kg by end of March
- **Key Milestones:** Double digits achieved (99.8kg, Week 8). Baker's Cyst managed. Progressive overload established.
- **End Condition:** Structural integrity confirmed, weight consistently under 97kg, Baker's Cyst cleared for impact (physio assessment recommended).
- **Key Programming:**
  - Pull-up progression in all upper body sessions (negatives, band-assisted, dead hangs)
  - Upper body plyo: med ball throws, explosive push-ups (2x/week)
  - Core stability: anti-extension, anti-rotation, anti-lateral flexion, hip hinge (3x/week)
  - Rower sprints: 20s MAX >300W / 1:40 rest (anaerobic protocol)
  - Stairmaster: 3-4 min Zone 4 intervals (aerobic high)
  - Conservative treadmill incline walking begins (pre-Phase 2 preparation)

### Phase 2: The Engine Build (Apr - Jun 2026)
- **Focus:** Introduce impact. Run/walk intervals. Grip intensification. Race prep.
- **Training:** Treadmill run/walk progression → outdoor running. Increase Stairmaster volume. Grip work: dead hangs, farmer carries, monkey bars. Continue pull-up progression.
- **Weight Target:** ~92-93kg
- **Protein:** Increases to 190g/day when weight drops below 95kg
- **Milestone:** Spartan Zandvoort Super (May 2026) — systems check. Walk most of it, test obstacles, practice penalties.
- **Key Transition:** Phase 1 → Phase 2 when Baker's Cyst is stable under impact and weight is under 97kg.
- **Running Introduction Protocol:**
  1. Weeks 1-2: Treadmill incline walking (10-15% grade, 4-5 km/h, 20 min)
  2. Weeks 3-4: Walk/jog intervals (2 min walk / 1 min jog, 20 min)
  3. Weeks 5-6: Walk/jog (1 min walk / 2 min jog, 25 min)
  4. Weeks 7-8: Jog with walk breaks (5 min jog / 1 min walk, 30 min)
  5. Beyond: Duration before speed. 10% weekly volume increase rule.
  - ALL on treadmill until Baker's Cyst confirmed stable. Outdoor transition after 4+ weeks symptom-free.
- **Lower Body Plyo Gate:** Weight <93kg + Baker's Cyst zero symptoms 6+ weeks + completed 4-week low-impact progression + can squat bodyweight x5.

### Phase 3: Dad Mode (Jul - Oct 2026)
- **Focus:** Minimum effective dose. Maintain, don't regress. Baby #3 arrives August.
- **Training:** 3x/week max. Home-based: Concept 2 rower + kettlebells (16kg, 20kg) + bodyweight. Gym when possible.
- **Calories:** Increase to maintenance (2,600-2,700 kcal). Protein stays at 190g/day. Do NOT cut during newborn sleep deprivation.
- **Schedule:** Week 1 post-birth = deload (movement only, focus on family). Week 2 = back to modified training.
- **Weight Target:** Hold 92-93kg. Hard ceiling: do NOT exceed 95kg.
- **Equipment:** C2 Rower is the backbone. 20-30min sessions. KB complexes for strength maintenance. Pull-up bar at home (if possible).
- **Mental Framework:** "3x/week is enough. You are not regressing — you are investing in longevity." Partner protocol: share reduced schedule in advance.
- **DEXA Scan #2:** November 2026. Assess Dad Mode impact on lean mass.

### Phase 4: The Winter Forge (Nov 2026 - Feb 2027)
- **Focus:** Hyper-focus training block. High volume Zone 2. Race weight push to 89kg. Endurance strength.
- **Training:** 5-6x/week. Long Zone 2 runs with Vizsla. Strength endurance circuits. Stairmaster volume. Heavy loaded carries.
- **Weight Target:** ~89kg (committed race weight)
- **Protein:** Increases to 200g/day when weight drops below 92kg. Calories: 2,400-2,500 (mild deficit, training volume creates gap).
- **Key Work:** Build the aerobic engine. Running volume ramp (10% rule). OCR-specific grip endurance. Race simulation sessions.
- **Assessment:** If DEXA #2 (Nov) confirms lean mass >70kg and sleep score consistently >70, evaluate 87kg stretch target.

### Phase 5: Mountain Specificity (Mar - May 2027)
- **Focus:** Vertical gain + grip. Mountain race preparation.
- **Training:** StairMaster high-volume (loaded). Dune/hill repeats. Heavy carry + running combos. Rope work (find facility or gym with rope). Monthly Limburg/Ardennes trips for real elevation.
- **Weight Target:** 87-89kg (race weight zone)
- **Calories:** Fuel for performance (2,700-3,000 kcal). No more deficit.
- **Optional:** Training race (Spartan Sprint/Super) for race-day systems check.
- **DEXA Scan #3:** May 2027. Final body composition validation.
- **Maurten Protocol Rehearsal:** Practice race-day fueling (60-90g carbs/hour) during long training sessions.
- **Mental Prep:** Visualization sessions. Practice suffering under fatigue. Long loaded stair sessions.

### Phase 6: Taper & Race (Jun 2027)
- **Focus:** Sharp and fresh. Peak performance.
- **Training:** Volume reduction 40-50%, intensity maintenance. Race-day nutrition rehearsal with Maurten. Mental rehearsal.
- **Race:** Spartan Ultra Morzine — July 5, 2027 (placeholder)
- **Target Race Weight:** 87-89kg
- **Mental Framework:** "Feeling undertrained during taper is the sign that taper is working."
