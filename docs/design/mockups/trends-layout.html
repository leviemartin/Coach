Key Design Decisions

AI Coaching Insight banner at the top — proactive, data-grounded insight from the coaching team. This is the differentiator nobody else has. Rotates based on what the coaches find most relevant right now.

Time ranges include "Journey" — shows the full span from journey start to now. No other platform does this. Gives athletes the "big picture" view of their entire training arc.

Chart selection is dynamic per athlete. An OCR racer sees Body Comp, Sleep, Strength, Nutrition, Training Load, Behavior Insights. A marathon runner might see Mileage, Pace Progression, Sleep, Nutrition, HR Zone Distribution, Behavior Insights. The coaching team determines which charts matter.

Behavior Insights Engine at the bottom — the WHOOP killer feature done better. Multi-variable correlations with confidence levels. Positive, negative, and emerging patterns. This is where the daily log + wearable + session data becomes genuinely actionable intelligence.

Strength progression shows ceiling history with category filtering. Athletes can see their entire progressive overload journey.
